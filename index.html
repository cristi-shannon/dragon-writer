Learn

View course data that automatically syncs with your SJSU Canvas account. The courses needed to complete your chosen program will appear as gates along the PTW Mountain. As you complete courses, the gates will turn from gray to gold.

Network

Compare your progress to other students in the program. Your legion represents which path in the program you will be taking: major or minor/certificate. Your legion determines which courses you will need to take as well as how long your journey may be. View leaderboards in the Coliseum to see how you measure up against the top 10 Spartans in your legion.

Succeed

Track the skills you accumulate both in and outside of your coursework. The dragon represents graduation. As you work your way up the mountain, you will gain the skills needed to take on and tame the dragon. Once you reach graduation, your dragon is tame enough to fly you out of the PTW Mountain and off to new horizons.

Basics

In your house, you can view your avatar and access external portfolio materials. In the Coliseum, you can find outside resources to help you gain skill points and check out a bulletin board for real-life events. In your settings, you can change your preferences.
